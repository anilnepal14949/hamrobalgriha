About HAMROGHAR

A well-established non-profitable and non-governmental organization to assist the orphan children and women from discriminated communities of Nepal. We help those children and women to get their basic needs and help reach their potential. "We care orphans because they deserve equal unconditional love and support to strengthen their beautiful future".

HamroGhar Balgriha, Hetauda (GJSSM Makwanpur) was established on 2008 A.D. as a non-profitable and non-governmental organization to assist the orphan children and women from discriminated communities of Nepal.

Our Mission

To care for orphans, the helpless, homeless, and poor in order for them to flourish into great future leaders by providing a safe shelter, free education, medical treatment, standard care, quality nutrition, and accountability for orphanages. Also, to raise the awareness programmes for orphans and marginalised girls and women against drugs, HIV/AIDS, violence, human and child trafficking, child labour and so on.

Core Principles

  • Work hard
  • Love children
  • Embrace accountability
  • Emphasize transparency and openness
  • Encourage dignity through promoting equity
  • Serve with humility
  • Speak out for individuals who are unable to do so
